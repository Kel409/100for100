# Project 2/100

## QuickBar :
"Inventory" Bar on the left to quickly store and retrieve images. Click shortcut (Ctrl-Alt-I) to open or open manager in taskbar to add/remove/close the program. 

In QuickBar, you can navigate with scroll wheel or UP and DOWN keys, press ENTER or double-click to copy image to clipboard.

## Changeables :

- sidebar (if you want to put in the effort)
